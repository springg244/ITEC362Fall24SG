Social Determinants of Health

The combination of individual
and environmental
factors that make some
individuals healthier than others.

Important Terms:

Community Health

Initiatives that aim to improve the health of specific populations

Epidemiology

The study of the spread of disease in relation to the social determinants of health

Infectious Disease

Illness contracted from a pathogen that can spread from host to host
