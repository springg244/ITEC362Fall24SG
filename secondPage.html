Featured Health Resources

Section 1: Physical Activity Recommendations


“...at least 150 minutes (2 hours and 30 minutes) to
300 minutes (5 hours) a week of moderate-intensity, or
75 minutes (1 hour and 15 minutes) to 150 minutes (2 hours and 30 minutes)
a week of vigorous-intensity aerobic physical activity"

  • Sedentary behavior increases the risk of frequent illness and heart disease

  • You can meet these guidelines in many different fun and creative ways

Section 2: Daily Dietary Recommendations

  • “Vegetables of all types
  • Fruits, especially whole fruit
  • Grains- at least half of which are whole-grain
  • Dairy- including fat-free or low-fat milk, yogurt, and cheese
  • Protein foods, including lean meats, poultry, and eggs;
    seafood; beans, peas, and lentils; and nuts, seeds, and soy product

  • Oils, including vegetable oils and oils in food, such as seafood and nuts”
  • Sodium limit: 2,300 milligrams per day.
  • Sugar should account for less than 10% of your daily calorie intake.
